Merchant Bankers - City-wise: New Delhi

The report presents the list of merchant bankers located in New Delhi. Only those merchant bankers who have managed Mainboard IPOs from 2007 onwards and SME IPOs from 2011 onwards are included in this report.

A merchant banker plays a crucial role throughout the IPO journey from the pre-IPO stage to post-listing activities. The issuing company works closely with the merchant banker for almost every IPO-related task, which requires continuous coordination and communication. Due to this close involvement, companies often prefer to appoint a merchant banker located near their operational base or within close geographical proximity.

Here you can see merchant bankers operating in New Delhi, total IPOs managed and their share of Mainboard and SME IPOs. Clicking on a merchant banker’s name shows the companies they managed, opening dates and where the shares are listed.
